You start broke. Here is how to fix that without gambling anything away.

## Free money, every day

| Command | Cooldown | Pays |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `/daily` | 24 hours | R10,000 base, more with a streak |
| `/work` | 5 minutes | R20,000 to R150,000 |
| `/beg` | 5 minutes | R5,000 to R50,000 |
| `/scratch` | 24 hours | Free scratch card, can win cash or items |
| `/vote` | per site | Vote for the bot on listing sites for rewards |

Too lazy to run them one by one? `/collect` claims everything that is ready in
one go, including business income and booster rewards.

## Daily streaks

Claim `/daily` every day and it grows by 10% per day. From day 7 onward you get
double, so R20,000 a day. Miss more than 48 hours and the streak resets, so
set a reminder.

## Keep it safe

Other players can `/rob` your **wallet**, never your bank. The habit to build
early:

1. Earn.
2. `/deposit all`.
3. Withdraw only what you plan to bet or spend.

## When you feel richer

Once your bank grows, riskier income unlocks: `/steal`, `/heist` and robbing
other players. Those can lose you money, so read
[Crime](/yumeko-docs/economy/crime/) first.
