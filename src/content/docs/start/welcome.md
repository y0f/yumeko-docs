Yumeko is a casino and economy bot for Discord. You earn a virtual currency
called **reds** (shown as `R`, like `R10,000`), and you can spend it on
gambling, businesses, crypto, fishing gear and more. It is all play money:
nothing costs or pays real cash.

## The basics

- **Wallet and bank.** Money in your wallet can be spent and stolen. Money in
  the bank is safe. Move it with `/deposit` and `/withdraw`.
- **Everything is a slash command.** Type `/` in Discord and look for Yumeko's
  icon. `/help` shows every command in the bot.
- **Typing amounts.** Anywhere the bot asks for an amount you can use
  shorthand: `10k` is R10,000, `1m` is R1,000,000, and `all` or `half` use your
  current balance.

## Your first five minutes

1. `/daily` for your first R10,000.
2. `/work` and `/beg` whenever they are off cooldown for steady income.
3. `/deposit all` so nobody robs you.
4. Try a small `/coinflip` or open the [games overview](/yumeko-docs/games/overview/).
5. Cast a fishing line with `/fish` and forget about it for a while.

## Handy commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/help` | Full command list inside Discord |
| `/wallet` and `/bank` | Check your balances |
| `/cooldowns` | See when your income commands are ready |
| `/stats` | Your money, gambling and level stats |
| `/leaderboard` | Top 10 richest players |
