## The lottery

A recurring lottery with real prize pools.

- Buy tickets with `/shop buy lottery-ticket`. More tickets, more chances.
- There is a cap on tickets per player per draw, so whales cannot simply buy
  the pot.
- The draw happens automatically on a fixed schedule and pays first, second
  and third prize.
- **Winnings go straight to your bank**, safe from robbers before you even
  wake up.

## Scratch cards

`/scratch` gives you one free card every 24 hours.

- Prizes range from nothing (most cards) to cash amounts and, on the luckiest
  cards, a collectible item jackpot.
- It is free. There is no reason to ever skip it. Add it to your daily
  routine along with `/daily` and `/collect`.
