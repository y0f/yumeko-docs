Fishing is Yumeko's idle game. You cast a line once and it keeps catching fish
by itself for up to about 30 minutes while you do literally anything else.

## The loop

1. `/fish` casts your line.
2. Chat, gamble, touch grass. The line fishes on its own.
3. `/fishing collect` scoops your catches into your backpack.
4. `/fishing sell` turns the whole backpack into cash.
5. Repeat.

## Useful commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/fishing status` | Is your line still running, what has it caught |
| `/fishing pause` / `resume` / `stop` | Control the line |
| `/fishing profile` | Your level, XP and gear |
| `/fishing backpack` | What you are carrying |
| `/fishing journal` | Your species collection |
| `/fishing help` | Everything in one embed |

## Leveling up

Every catch gives fishing XP. Levels unlock better rods and new fishing
zones with rarer fish. Rarities run from common up to **mythic**, and the
rare ones are where the money is.

## Daily bounties

`/fishing bounties` shows daily tasks (catch X of this, sell Y of that) that
pay cash and XP. They reset every day, grab them before you cast.
