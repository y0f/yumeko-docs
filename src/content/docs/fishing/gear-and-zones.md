Rod, bait, zone, weather and time of day all add to the same four bonuses:

- **Rare** raises the chance of a rarer, pricier fish.
- **Value** adds to what a catch sells for.
- **Multi-catch** (rods only) is the per-bite chance to land two fish.
- **XP** speeds up your fishing level.

## Bait

is crafted from spare fish with `/fishing craft`. The three priced lures are bought
with `/fishing buybait` (up to 1000 at a time); the rest are drop-only.

## Weather, time, hotspots

`/fishing weather` shows what is live. These stack on your gear.

| Weather | Rare | Value | XP |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sunny | +0% | +0% | +0% |
| Cloudy | +3% | +0% | +2% |
| Foggy | +10% | +6% | +6% |
| Light Rain | +8% | +5% | +5% |
| Thunderstorm | +18% | +12% | +10% |
| Aurora | +30% | +25% | +20% |

| Time | Rare | Value | XP |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day | +0% | +0% | +0% |
| Dawn | +12% | +6% | +8% |
| Dusk | +12% | +6% | +8% |
| Night | +18% | +10% | +12% |

Hotspots pop up at random and add +50% rare while active. Weekends add +25%, but
only to sell value.

## Journal

`/fishing journal` tracks every species caught, per zone. Completing a zone's list
pays a one-time reward. It is permanent even if you sell everything.
