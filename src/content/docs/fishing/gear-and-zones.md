## Rods

Buy rods in `/fishing shop` and equip them with `/fishing rod`. Better rods
need a minimum fishing level and raise your chance of rare catches. A rod is
the single biggest upgrade you can make, prioritize it.

## Bait

- Buy with `/fishing buybait` (up to 1000 per purchase), equip with
  `/fishing bait`.
- Craft bait from spare fish with `/fishing craft`, which turns junk catches
  into something useful.
- Bait boosts your odds while it lasts, keep it stocked for serious sessions.

## Zones and travel

`/fishing travel` moves you between fishing locations. Zones unlock as your
fishing level grows. Each zone has its own species pool and its own **sell
bonuses**, so where you fish and where you sell both matter.

## Weather and hotspots

`/fishing weather` shows current conditions. Certain weather and temporary
hotspots boost rare catch chances. If the weather is good, that is the moment
to burn your best bait.

## The journal

`/fishing journal` tracks every species you have ever caught, per zone.
Completing a zone's species list pays a one-time reward, and the journal is
permanent even if you sell everything.
