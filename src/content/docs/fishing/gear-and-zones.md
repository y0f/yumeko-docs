Rod, bait, zone, weather and time of day all add to the same four bonuses:

- **Rare** raises the chance of a rarer, pricier fish.
- **Value** adds to what a catch sells for.
- **Multi-catch** (rods only) is the per-bite chance to land two fish.
- **XP** speeds up your fishing level.

## Rods

Buy in `/fishing shop`, equip with `/fishing rod`. Each tier needs a higher level.

## Bait

Equip with `/fishing bait`. Cheap common bait drops free while you fish (about 1
catch in 5) or is crafted from spare fish with `/fishing craft`, so you rarely need
to buy the low tiers. The stronger lures are bought with `/fishing buybait` (up to
1000 at a time).

Everything up to Glowworm can drop free while you fish; the lures above it are
buy-only. Bait is consumed one per cast and priced at about what the extra value and
rare catches it brings are worth at the top zone, so it roughly pays for itself. It
is a small edge for chasing rare fish and filling your journal, not free profit.

| Bait | Value | Rare | XP | Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Spinner Bait | +1% | +2% | +2% | 100 |
| Plankton Cloud | +3% | +4% | +3% | 250 |
| Glowworm | +4% | +5% | +4% | 350 |
| Magic Lure | +6% | +8% | +5% | 550 |
| Chum Bucket | +7% | +9% | +6% | 600 |
| Golden Bait | +9% | +11% | +8% | 800 |
| Void Pellet | +10% | +13% | +9% | 900 |
| Celestial Bait | +12% | +15% | +12% | 1,050 |

The rare bonus is the real draw on the top lures: it tilts you toward the rarest,
priciest fish. Roe, a common drop, biases the pool toward salmon.

## Zones

`/fishing travel` moves between zones, each with its own species pool. Fish are
valued at your current zone's sell bonus at the moment you sell, not where you
caught them, but the bonus is small everywhere, so there is no reason to haul a
catch to another zone to sell it. Higher zones pay off through better fish, not a
bigger sell bonus.

| Zone | Level | Sell bonus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tranquil Pond | 1 | +0% |
| Winding River | 5 | +1% |
| Misty Lake | 12 | +2% |
| Open Ocean | 22 | +3% |
| Coral Reef | 32 | +4% |
| The Deep | 45 | +5% |
| Sunken City | 60 | +6% |
| Celestial Rift | 80 | +8% |

## Weather, time, hotspots

`/fishing weather` shows what is live. These stack on your gear.

| Weather | Rare | Value | XP |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sunny | +0% | +0% | +0% |
| Cloudy | +3% | +0% | +2% |
| Foggy | +10% | +6% | +6% |
| Light Rain | +8% | +5% | +5% |
| Thunderstorm | +18% | +12% | +10% |
| Aurora | +30% | +25% | +20% |

| Time | Rare | Value | XP |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day | +0% | +0% | +0% |
| Dawn | +12% | +6% | +8% |
| Dusk | +12% | +6% | +8% |
| Night | +18% | +10% | +12% |

Hotspots pop up at random and add +50% rare while active. Weekends add +25%, but
only to sell value.

## Journal

`/fishing journal` tracks every species caught, per zone. Completing a zone's list
pays a one-time reward. It is permanent even if you sell everything.
