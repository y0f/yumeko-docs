Sometimes a boss fish strikes your idle line. This is the one part of fishing
that is not idle: you have to show up and fight.

## The fight

- You get a **5-minute window** to respond before it escapes.
- The fight plays over rounds of button presses. Each reel attempt has
  roughly a 75% chance to succeed.
- Fail a reel and your line strains. **Three snaps and the boss escapes.**

## Why bother

Boss fish are worth far more than normal catches, in cash, XP and journal
prestige. If your phone buzzes because something enormous grabbed your line,
answer it.

## Tips

- Keep notifications on for the channel where you fish.
- A better rod helps before the fight even starts, by making boss strikes more
  likely in the first place.
