Yumeko has a play-money crypto market with 20 coins (BTC, ETH and friends,
down to stablecoins) tracking live real-world prices. No real money is
involved, you trade with reds.

## Commands

All under `/crypto`:

| Action | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `prices` | Current prices for all 20 coins |
| `buy` | Spend reds on a coin |
| `sell` | Sell coins back to reds |
| `portfolio` | Your holdings with profit and loss |
| `history` | Your past trades |

## The fine print

- **0.5% fee** on every buy and every sell.
- Prices move on their own, you can genuinely lose (or gain) value by holding.
- Your crypto counts toward the wealth `/leaderboard`, so it is not a hiding
  spot, but it is also rob-proof like the bank.

## Quick tips

- Fees are round trips: buy plus sell costs about 1%. Small scalps rarely pay.
- Stablecoins barely move, they are basically a second bank account with a fee.
- Check `portfolio` before selling, it shows your profit and loss per coin.
