Crime pays big and fails often. Every job needs a minimum wallet balance, so
you always have something to lose. That is the point.

## /steal

Pickpocket a random NPC.

- **Cooldown:** 15 minutes. **Needs:** 500 in your wallet.
- **55% success:** win 500 to 10,000.
- **Fail:** pay a 500 fine.
- Small bonus: 5% chance to find a Bank Heist Card when you succeed.

## /rob

Rob a real player's wallet.

- **Cooldown:** 30 minutes. **Needs:** you and the target both hold at least
  1,000 in wallet.
- **40% success:** take 5% to 15% of their wallet.
- **Fail:** you pay them 10% of your wallet. Embarrassing.
- Targets holding a **Rob Shield** cannot be robbed.

## /heist

Solo bank job, high stakes.

- **Cooldown:** 4 hours. **Needs:** 5,000 in your wallet.
- **35% success:** win 10,000 to 75,000.
- **Fail:** pay 5,000 bail.
- 10% chance to find a Bank Heist Card on success.

## /bankheist (co-op)

Team job for 2 to 4 players. Someone spends a **Bank Heist Card** to start,
everyone needs at least 10,000 in wallet.

- **50% success**, everyone wins or everyone goes down together.
- **Payout per player:** 2 players 50k-150k, 3 players 100k-300k,
  4 players 200k-600k.
- Winners always get at least one Silver Bar, with a chance at rarer
  collectibles: Gold Bar, Diamond, Ancient Artifact, even Crown Jewels.
- **Fail:** 10,000 bail each.

Cards come from the shop (Heist Kit), or as lucky drops from `/steal` and
`/heist`.
