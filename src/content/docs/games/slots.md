Both machines use a fixed bet ladder instead of free bet amounts:
**1,000 / 5,000 / 10,000 / 25,000 / 50,000**.

## Neko Strike (`/slots neko`)

A collector slot. **Money symbols** land with values on them, a **Collect
symbol** scoops everything on screen. Money plus Collect together trigger
**respins** where the reels lock and refill, stacking value for one big scoop.

- Maximum win: **20,000x your bet** in a single game.
- **Bonus buy:** skip straight to the feature. Respins cost 200x your stake,
  Super Respins 400x.

## Grave Reels (`/slots grave`)

A 5x3 graveyard slot that pays by **symbol count** anywhere on screen: 6 or
more of a symbol pays, wilds count toward every symbol at once.

- The tombstone tier symbols pay best, up to roughly 1000x for a full screen.
- Land **3 or more coffin scatters** for a pick-a-box bonus round.

## Honest warning

Slots are the swingiest games in the bot. Long dry streaks are normal and the
huge multipliers are rare. Set a budget per session, the machine will not do
it for you.
