Drop a ball down a peg board. It bounces left and right at random and lands in
a multiplier bucket at the bottom. The middle buckets pay little (or less than
your bet), the edges pay big.

## Risk levels

- **Low:** gentle buckets, small swings, good for long sessions.
- **Medium:** middle ground.
- **High:** the middle buckets get bleak and the edge buckets get huge. Most
  drops lose, one lucky bounce pays for many.

## Betting

- 1 minimum, 1,000,000 maximum.
- After each drop you get quick re-bet buttons: half, double, max, or a custom
  amount, so you can chain drops fast.

## Tips

- The ball physics are pure chance, there is no timing trick.
- High risk works like a slot machine: budget a number of drops and stop when
  they are gone.
