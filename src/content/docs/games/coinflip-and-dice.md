## /coinflip

Pick heads or tails, bet anything from R1 to **R5,000,000**.

- Win: **2x** your bet.
- Lose: bet gone.
- That is the whole game. Fifty-fifty, instant result.

## /dice

You and the bot each roll a die. Higher roll wins.

- Win: **2x** your bet.
- Tie: your bet comes back, no harm done.
- Lose: bet gone.
- Same R5,000,000 max as coinflip.

### Loaded Dice

The **Loaded Dice** item (from the shop) tilts your next dice game: your roll
leans high, the bot's leans low. It only works on bets up to R25,000, so no
free millions.

## Why play these

Highest bet caps in the bot and the fastest way to double up (or halve down).
If you just want action without reading rules, start here.
