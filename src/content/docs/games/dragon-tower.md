A tower with **9 floors**. Each floor has a row of doors, and behind some of
them waits a dragon. Pick a safe door to climb, hit a dragon and your bet is
gone. Cash out after any floor to bank the current multiplier.

## Difficulties

| Difficulty | Doors per floor | Dragons | Feel |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Easy | 4 | 1 | 75% safe per floor |
| Medium | 3 | 1 | 67% safe |
| Hard | 2 | 1 | Coinflip every floor |
| Expert | 3 | 2 | 33% safe |
| Master | 4 | 3 | 25% safe, reach the top for a fortune |

## The odds

Payouts are the true odds with only a **0.5% house cut**, the fairest game in
the bot. Every floor multiplies your payout by the honest risk you took, so a
full Master climb pays enormous multiples.

## Tips

- Easy and Medium are grinding modes: cash out around floors 4 to 6.
- Hard and up are for sending it. A full 9-floor Hard run is nine coinflips in
  a row, treat it accordingly.
- Same rule as Mines: pick your exit floor before you start climbing.
