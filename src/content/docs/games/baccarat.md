Baccarat deals two hands, **Player** and **Banker**, by fixed casino rules.
You just pick which side wins. No decisions after the bet, which makes it the
most relaxing table in the bot.

## Payouts

| Your bet | Pays | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Player | 2.0x | |
| Banker | 1.95x | Banker wins slightly more often, hence the trim |
| Tie | 9.0x | Rare, big payout |

If you bet Player or Banker and the hands tie, your bet is returned.

## How the hands work

Cards count by their number, tens and face cards count zero, and only the last
digit of the total matters (7 + 8 = 15 = 5). Closest to 9 wins. Third-card
draws follow automatic rules, the bot handles all of it.

## Quick tips

- Banker is statistically the safest bet in the game, even after the 1.95x
  trim.
- Tie looks juicy at 9x but hits rarely. Treat it as a lottery ticket, not a
  strategy.
