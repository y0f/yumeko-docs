`/monopoly` (and its variant `/monopoly-q`) plays like bingo: you get a card,
numbered balls are drawn, and matching lines and patterns pay out.

## Card types

- **Free Space card:** the center square is marked for free, so lines complete
  more easily. Steadier, smaller wins.
- **Chance / Gold card:** the center square holds a multiplier instead of a
  free mark. Harder to complete lines, bigger payouts when you do.

## Bonus balls

Some drawn balls are special:

- Bonus balls worth **10x or 20x**, or **20x to 50x** when they complete a
  line.
- Global multipliers of **2x to 4x** that boost the entire card's winnings.

## Betting

Standard limits: R1 up to R1,000,000.

## Tips

- Free Space is the beginner mode, Chance/Gold is for chasing spikes.
- Payouts stack: a line finished by a bonus ball under a global multiplier is
  the dream scenario.
