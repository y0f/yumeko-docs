One spin, one result. Most slices pay small or nothing, a few pay well, and
one slice sends you to a bonus wheel where the real money lives.

## Main wheel

Out of 100 slices:

| Result | Slices |
| --- | --- |
| 0x (nothing) | 42 |
| 0.2x | 20 |
| 0.9x | 20 |
| 2.4x | 12 |
| 4.3x | 5 |
| BONUS | 1 |

## Bonus wheel

Hit the 1-in-100 BONUS slice and you spin again for **5x, 10x, 20x, 40x or
80x**, plus a 1% shot at **SUPER**.

## SUPER

The SUPER round is a pick-a-box game with prizes reaching into hundreds of
times your bet. It is the rarest event in the bot: 1 in 100 for bonus, then
1 in 100 again for SUPER.

## Honest warning

The wheel keeps a bigger cut than most games here (roughly 12% on average).
Nearly half of all spins pay zero. Play it for the bonus chase, not for
steady profit. Grinders should look at
[Dragon Tower](/yumeko-docs/games/dragon-tower/) or [Mines](/yumeko-docs/games/mines/) instead.
