Crash is played together. A multiplier starts at 1x and climbs. At a random
moment it crashes. Everyone who cashed out before the crash keeps their
multiplier, everyone still riding loses their bet.

## How a round works

1. A **15-second betting window** opens. Join with the quick buttons (R100,
   R500, R1k, R5k) or a custom amount.
2. The rocket launches and the multiplier climbs.
3. Hit **cash out** any time to lock your bet times the current multiplier.
4. Crash. Survivors paid, riders rekt, next round.

## The numbers

- About **7% of rounds crash instantly** at 1x. Nothing you can do about
  those.
- The multiplier can theoretically reach **10,000x**.
- On average the house keeps about 7%.

## Tips

- Cashing out around 1.5x to 2x wins often but slowly. Riding to 10x wins
  rarely but big. Same average either way, pick your flavor.
- The fun is collective: everyone sees who bailed at 1.2x and who rode to the
  moon. Or into the ground.
