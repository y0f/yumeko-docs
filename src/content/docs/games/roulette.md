`/roulette` opens a shared table anyone in the channel can join. A **30-second
betting window** starts, everyone builds their own slip of bets, then a single
wheel spin resolves the whole table at once. It is a European single-zero wheel:
37 pockets, 0 to 36, one green zero.

## How to bet

Click **Place bet** to open your **private chip tray** (only you see it):

1. Pick a chip value once (R100, R500, R1k, R10k, or a custom amount).
2. Tap a bet to place that chip on it. No typing, no menus.
3. Stack as many bets as you like. **Undo** and **Clear** are free until the spin.

Your bets stay private, and no money leaves your wallet until the table closes,
so you can build and edit your slip freely.

## Payouts

Every payout is the total you get back, stake included, so 2x doubles your money.

| Bet | Covers | Pays |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Single number (straight) | 1 pocket | 36x |
| Dozen (1-12, 13-24, 25-36) | 12 | 3x |
| Column | 12 | 3x |
| Red / Black | 18 | 2x |
| Odd / Even | 18 | 2x |
| 1-18 / 19-36 | 18 | 2x |

## Reading the odds

Every bet keeps the house the exact same cut: **2.70%**, the single green zero.
Cover more of the wheel and you win more often but pay less; cover one number
and you win 1 spin in 37 but pay 36x. There is no secretly "best" bet, only how
spicy you want it.

The zero is the whole edge. When 0 hits, every outside bet (colors, dozens,
columns, odd/even, high/low) loses. Only a straight bet on 0 itself wins.

## Tips

- Hedging Red and Black on the same spin does not beat the house, it just hands
  the edge to the zero, so both lose when 0 lands.
- A single number is the big-spike play: 36x total, but a long dry run between
  hits. The even-money bets are the low-variance grind.
- Everyone at the table bets their own slip, so this is where the group trash
  talk lives, like [Crash](/docs/games/crash/) and
  [Horse Racing](/docs/games/horse-racing/).
