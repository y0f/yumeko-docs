Your chicken crosses a road step by step. Every step multiplies your payout a
little more, and every step has a chance of going splat. Cash out any time to
keep what you have earned.

## Difficulties

| Difficulty | Steps | Danger per step |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Easy | 24 | 6% |
| Medium | 22 | 10% |
| Hard | 20 | 15% |
| Hardcore | 15 | 25% |

Harder roads start with a bigger multiplier and grow much faster per step.
Hardcore starts at 1.4x and grows 30% every single step, which is why a full
crossing pays absurd amounts and almost never happens.

## Tips

- Easy mode grows slowly. It only beats the other modes if you walk far, so
  do not cash out after two steps.
- Hardcore is the classic "double a few times then run" game: three or four
  steps in, the multiplier is already juicy and the odds of dying are real.
