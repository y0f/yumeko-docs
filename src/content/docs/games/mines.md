A grid hides diamonds and mines. Flip tiles one by one: every diamond raises
your multiplier, one mine ends the game and takes your bet. Cash out any time
to lock in the current multiplier.

## Setup

- Grid: **3x3** (9 tiles) or **4x4** (16 tiles).
- Mines: you choose, from 1 up to one less than the tile count.
- More mines = bigger multipliers per safe tile = faster death.

## How the multiplier works

The payout is the true odds of surviving your picks, minus a **1% house cut**.
So a 99% fair game: the multiplier is exactly as dangerous as it looks.

A few feels-like examples on 4x4:

- 3 mines, cash out after 4 safe tiles: modest but steady.
- 5 mines, survive 5 tiles: several times your bet.
- 15 mines on 4x4 (one safe tile): the single-flip jackpot.

## Tips

- Decide your cash-out point **before** you start. The game is designed to
  make "one more tile" feel cheap.
- Low mine counts are for grinding, high mine counts are lottery tickets.
