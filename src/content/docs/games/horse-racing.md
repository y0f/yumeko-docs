`/horse` starts a race everyone in the channel can bet on. A **15-second
betting window** opens, you pick a horse and an amount (R1 minimum), then the
race plays out live.

## The field

| Horse | Pays | Win chance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jerry | 1.9x | 50% |
| Vlippiono | 4.5x | 20% |
| Rickiaci | 6.5x | 14% |
| Eliano | 9.0x | 10% |
| Angelito | 15.0x | 6% |

## Reading the odds

Jerry wins half of all races and pays just under double. Angelito wins 1 race
in 17 and pays 15x. Every horse keeps roughly the same house cut, so there is
no secretly "best" pick, only how spicy you want it.

## Tips

- Multiple people can bet on different horses in the same race, which is
  where the trash talk comes from.
- Betting Jerry every race is the low-variance grind. Betting Angelito every
  race is a personality trait.
